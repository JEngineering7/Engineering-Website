Week of 9/26/22: This week, I started to learn Bioengineering. The goal this week is to model a prosthetic for an animal. Restricted animals include Dog, Cat, Turtle, and Human prosthetics. I used a Polar Bear with the hind leg as the prosthetic. The main materials used are wood and string for the snow shoe, Titanium alloys for the main cylinders and ball joints,industrial lubricant for the ball joints, Polyurethane Laminate connecting the cylnders and covering any exposed parts including the snow shoes. Three ball joints are required. I would give the prosthetic at $5,372.
